what formuler to use for this.                                                  
                                                                                
The general idea is the ThreadRegistry gets the larger chunk of threads for its 
 use then 1-2 or 30% of the number of threads gets giving to                    
BackgroundJobRegistry.

actually spawn a thread, instead it will call to the BackgrounfJobRegistry to   
submit a task and gets a Result<()> indicating if task was submitted into the   
queue and thats it.

 however it wants.
